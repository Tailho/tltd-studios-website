# ECOSYSTEM

## TLTD Ecosystem
TLTD’s ecosystem is a connected platform of built environment and business services designed to support clients, collaborators, and communities.

## Marketplace
A curated marketplace for professional services, design resources, and construction partners.

## Business Directory
A searchable directory of trusted local architects, builders, developers, and consultants.

## Professional Network
A network for industry professionals to collaborate, share expertise, and access opportunities.

## Education
Learning resources, workshops, and training focused on design, construction, entrepreneurship, and leadership.

## Funding
Guidance and access to funding sources, investment advisory, and project financing support.

## Community
A platform for local voices, partner stories, and collaborative development initiatives.

## Job Board
A focused job board for construction, architecture, design, media, and tech roles.

## Tender Portal
A portal for public and private tender opportunities, bids, and project partnerships.

## Digital Products
Templates, guides, and tools that help clients plan, present, and manage their projects.
