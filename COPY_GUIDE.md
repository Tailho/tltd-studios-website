# COPY GUIDE

## Headline Structure
- Clear benefit-driven headlines.
- Short and memorable.
- Use active language.

## CTA Style
- Direct and confident.
- Use verbs like “Start,” “Explore,” “Build,” “Speak,” “Discuss.”
- Keep CTAs concise.

## Button Wording
- Use simple, action-first phrases.
- Keep button labels under four words.
- Examples: “Start a Project,” “View Services,” “Book a Consultation.”

## Tone
- Premium and approachable.
- Practical, not flashy.
- Educative and purposeful.

## Microcopy
- Use helpful, concise explanations.
- Support user decisions with trust signals.
- Keep form and interface copy minimal and clear.
