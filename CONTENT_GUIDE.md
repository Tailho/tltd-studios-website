# CONTENT GUIDE

## Tone and Style
- Never use buzzwords.
- Never sound corporate.
- Always sound premium.
- Always educate.
- Always inspire.
- Professional.
- Architectural.
- Minimal.
- Confident.

## Writing Principles
- Prioritize clarity over complexity.
- Use active voice.
- Make every sentence purposeful.
- Keep paragraphs short and scannable.

## Content Goals
- Communicate value quickly.
- Build trust with evidence and experience.
- Encourage thoughtful action.
- Reflect local relevance and regional expertise.
