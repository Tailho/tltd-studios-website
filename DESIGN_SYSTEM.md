# DESIGN SYSTEM

## Brand Colours
- Primary: rich black, charcoal, warm white
- Accent: gold, terracotta, forest green, slate blue
- Neutral palette for typography and surfaces

## Typography
- Primary font: premium sans-serif for headlines
- Secondary font: refined serif or geometric sans-serif for body copy
- Clear hierarchy with strong contrast and spacing

## Spacing
- Modular spacing scale for consistent layouts
- Use generous white space to reinforce premium positioning

## Border Radius
- Sharp and subtle rounded corners for cards and buttons
- Maintain a refined and modern aesthetic

## Buttons
- Primary button states: filled dark, hover accent, disabled subdued
- Secondary buttons: outlined or ghost style
- Clear CTA hierarchy

## Cards
- Minimal, layered cards with subtle shadows and glass effects
- Consistent padding, rounded edges, and imagery treatment

## Glassmorphism
- Used sparingly for premium overlays and hero details
- Soft blur, subtle transparency, and layered depth

## Animation Timing
- Smooth ease curves for hover states and transitions
- Use 150–320ms durations for micro-interactions and page elements

## Grid
- Responsive 12-column grid for desktop
- Stacked layouts for tablet and mobile
- Maintain balanced margins and gutter spacing

## Icons
- Clean, custom icon set
- Line-based or bold glyphs with minimal detail
- Consistent stroke weight and spacing

## Image Style
- High-quality photography with architectural composition
- Balanced contrast and natural light

## Photography Style
- Authentic, aspirational imagery of spaces, people, and projects
- Focus on environment, craft, materiality, and community

## Illustration Style
- Minimal, modern illustration with premium accents
- Use geometry, linework, and refined color palettes

## Dark Theme
- Dark surfaces with bright text contrast
- Use accent color sparingly to maintain elegance

## Light Theme
- Clean bright surfaces with warm neutrals
- Crisp contrast and refined tonal transitions

## Accessibility Rules
- Maintain WCAG AA color contrast
- Support keyboard navigation and clear focus states
- Use semantic elements and accessible text labels
